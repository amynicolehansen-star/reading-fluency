**Reading Level:** 200L
**Grade:** 1st

**COMPREHENSION QUESTIONS:**

1. What color is the ball that Max finds?

2. Who plays with Max?

3. What does Max do when he is tired?

4. How do you think Max feels about playing ball? Why?
