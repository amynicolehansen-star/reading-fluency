**Genre:** Fiction
**Reading Level:** 300L
**Grade:** 1st

---

```

a secret plan.                                                                 18

the surprise.                                                                  125

"Happy birthday, Mom!" they shout together.                                   131

Mom smiles so big. "This is the best birthday surprise ever!" she says. She  145
gives Jake and Anna huge hugs.                                                151

Jake feels proud. He made Mom's birthday special!                             159
```

**COMPREHENSION QUESTIONS:**

1. Whose birthday is it in the story?

2. What does Jake make for breakfast?

3. Who helps Jake with the surprise?

4. Why does Jake feel proud at the end of the story?
