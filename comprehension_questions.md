# Comprehension Questions - 490L Reading Passages

---

## Passage 1: The Secret Garden Path (Fiction)

1. What led Maya to discover the hidden gate?

2. Describe what Maya found at the end of the stone path.

3. How did Maya feel about her discovery? Use evidence from the text to support your answer.

4. Why do you think the author calls this Maya's "secret garden"?

---

## Passage 2: How Butterflies Grow (Nonfiction)

1. What are the four stages of a butterfly's life cycle?

2. Why does a caterpillar shed its skin several times?

3. What happens inside the chrysalis during the third stage?

4. Why can't a butterfly fly immediately after emerging from the chrysalis?

---

## Passage 3: The Winning Shot (Fiction)

1. What difficult decision did Carlos have to make during the game?

2. What advice from Coach Martinez helped Carlos make his decision?

3. Describe what happened after Josh released the ball toward the hoop.

4. How do you think Carlos felt at the end of the story? Explain your reasoning.

---

## Passage 4: The Amazing Arctic Fox (Nonfiction)

1. Name two physical adaptations that help the Arctic fox survive in cold temperatures.

2. How does the Arctic fox's fur change between winter and summer, and why?

3. What does it mean that the Arctic fox is an "opportunistic eater"?

4. How does the Arctic fox find food during the winter months?

---

## Passage 5: The Piano Recital (Fiction)

1. How did Emma feel before her performance? Use details from the text.

2. What technique did Mrs. Rodriguez teach Emma that helped her during the recital?

3. What does the author mean when she writes that Emma's confidence was "melting away like ice cream on a hot day"?

4. How did Emma's feelings change from the beginning to the end of the passage?

---

## Passage 6: Ancient Egyptian Pyramids (Nonfiction)

1. What was the main purpose of the pyramids?

2. Describe two challenges that workers faced when building the pyramids.

3. How did ancient Egyptian builders demonstrate their knowledge of mathematics and astronomy?

4. According to the passage, approximately how many stone blocks were used to build the Great Pyramid?
